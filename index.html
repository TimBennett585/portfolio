About Tim

After finishing my degree in Music Business from SUNY Fredonia, I worked with several smaller touring bands from NY and even into Nebraska. I served as their Tour Manager, keeping track of the financials, contacts, lodging, routing, well basically everything that kept the tour moving productively and on time!!

Having done that for several years, it was increasinglt obvious that to make that a larger career, I would need to invest all of my time for years on making that dream happen, and there would still be no guarantees. I then shifted focus into my part-time retail job with the Sherwin-Williams Paint Company and worked my way up to being a Training Store Manager, where I celebrated more than a decade with the company.

But, like many, after COVID hit, I re-evaluated my priorities and I knew that a change needed to be made. After soul-searching and discussing with friends and loved ones, I decided in 2023 to make the leap into learning programming and development and enrolled with Thinkful. I recently completed the program and wrapped up several projects, and have embraced the challenge of learning so many new things. I am truly excited to see what the future holds and am fully committed to the grind!!
